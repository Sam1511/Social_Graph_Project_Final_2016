Welcome to Graduate Admission at Denmark Technical University, one of the world’s leading research universities. DTC is home to over 24,000 top-caliber graduate and professional students —more than half the entire DTC student population— who represent all 50 states and 135 countries around the world. We are proud to host one of the largest numbers of international students Denmark. We offer over 100 highly regarded graduate and professional programs in our liberal arts college and 18 professional schools. We encourage you to review our Programs to learn more about the graduate student experience at DTU and to discover which of our academic programs is best suited for your interests.
